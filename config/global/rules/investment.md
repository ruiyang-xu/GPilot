# Investment Analysis Rules

## Valuation Framework

- Always state the valuation methodology used (DCF, comps, precedent transactions, etc.)
- Flag confidence level: High / Medium / Low with reasoning
- For private companies, use multiple methods and triangulate
- For public companies, include both absolute (DCF) and relative (comps) valuations
- Always include bear/base/bull scenarios with probability weights

## Deal Screening

- Check size fits fund parameters ($500K–$20M depending on stage)
- Verify sector alignment (Software/SaaS, AI/ML, Fintech, Healthtech, Deeptech)
- Flag any conflicts of interest
- Note co-investor quality and lead investor reputation

## Due Diligence

- Financial DD: Revenue quality, unit economics, burn rate, runway
- Technical DD: Moat defensibility, tech differentiation, IP position
- Market DD: TAM/SAM/SOM, competitive landscape, market timing
- Team DD: Founder-market fit, team completeness, retention

## Reporting Standards

- Use ILPA standards for LP reporting
- ASC 820 for fair value measurements
- Follow GAAP for fund accounting
