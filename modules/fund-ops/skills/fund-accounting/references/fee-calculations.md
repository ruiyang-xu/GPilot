# Fee Calculation Formulas

## Management Fee

### During Investment Period (Years 1-5)
```
Annual Fee = Committed Capital × 2.0%
Quarterly Fee = Annual Fee / 4
Monthly Fee = Annual Fee / 12
```

**Example**: $100M committed capital
- Annual fee: $100M × 2% = $2.0M
- Quarterly: $500K
- Monthly: ~$166.7K

### Post-Investment Period (Years 6-10+)
```
Annual Fee = Invested Capital (at cost) × 2.0%
```
"Invested capital at cost" = total capital invested in portfolio companies still held (excludes realized investments and write-offs).

**Example**: $80M invested, $20M realized
- Fee base: $60M (still held at cost)
- Annual fee: $60M × 2% = $1.2M

### Fee Timing
- Typically charged quarterly in advance
- First fee period may be pro-rated from final close date
- Fee accrues from date of each LP closing (for multiple closings)

## Organizational Expenses

- One-time costs of fund formation: legal, accounting, regulatory filings, placement agent fees
- **Typical cap**: $500K-$1M (check LPA)
- Amortized over investment period or charged in Year 1
- Amounts over cap borne by GP personally

## Fund Operating Expenses

Charged to the fund (not covered by management fee):
| Expense | Typical Annual | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Audit fees | $30K-$75K | Required annually |
| Legal fees | $25K-$75K | Fund-level legal matters |
| Tax preparation | $20K-$50K | Fund K-1s and returns |
| Fund administration | $30K-$100K | NAV, capital accounts, reporting |
| Insurance (D&O, E&O) | $15K-$40K | Fund-level coverage |
| Regulatory filings | $5K-$15K | Form ADV, Form PF, blue sky |
| Bank/custody fees | $5K-$15K | Account maintenance |
| Travel (deal-related) | Variable | Some funds include, some exclude |
| **Total estimate** | **$130K-$370K/yr** | |

## Fee Offset
If GP receives monitoring or transaction fees from portfolio companies, these typically offset management fees:
- Common offset: 80-100% of such fees credited against management fee
- Check LPA for specific offset percentage

## Worked Example — Year 1

**Fund**: $100M committed, first close January 1
**Capital called**: $15M (investments) + $2M (fees/expenses) = $17M

| Line Item | Q1 | Q2 | Q3 | Q4 | Annual |
|-----------|-----|-----|-----|-----|--------|
| Fee Base | $100M | $100M | $100M | $100M | $100M |
| Mgmt Fee | $500K | $500K | $500K | $500K | $2.0M |
| Fund Expenses | $40K | $30K | $30K | $50K | $150K |
| Org Costs (amort) | $100K | $100K | $100K | $100K | $400K |
| **Total Charges** | **$640K** | **$630K** | **$630K** | **$650K** | **$2.55M** |

Available for investment: $100M - $2.55M (Y1 fees) = ~$97.45M (Year 1), declining each year.
Over fund life: typically 85-90% of committed capital actually invested in companies.
