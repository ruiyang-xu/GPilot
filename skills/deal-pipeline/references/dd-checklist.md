# Due Diligence Checklist

Depth scales with stage: **Seed** = light DD (1-2 weeks), **Series A** = standard DD (2-4 weeks), **Series B+** = full DD (4-8 weeks).

---

## 1. Commercial Due Diligence

### Market Analysis
- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM with bottoms-up and top-down sizing
- [ ] Market growth rate and key drivers
- [ ] Regulatory environment and upcoming changes
- [ ] "Why now?" — catalysts enabling this company
- [ ] Market map: key players, segments, value chain

### Competitive Landscape
- [ ] Direct competitors (feature comparison matrix)
- [ ] Indirect competitors and substitutes
- [ ] Competitive moat assessment (network effects, switching costs, data advantages, IP)
- [ ] Win/loss analysis (why do customers choose/reject this product?)
- [ ] Competitor funding and trajectory

### Customers
- [ ] Customer references (3-5 calls for Series A+)
- [ ] Customer concentration risk (top 10 customers % of revenue)
- [ ] NPS or satisfaction scores
- [ ] Expansion revenue and upsell potential
- [ ] Churn analysis (logo and revenue churn, cohort analysis)

### Business Model
- [ ] Revenue model clarity and predictability
- [ ] Pricing strategy and power
- [ ] Sales cycle length and motion (PLG, inside sales, enterprise)
- [ ] Channel strategy and partnerships

---

## 2. Technical Due Diligence

### Product
- [ ] Product demo and hands-on evaluation
- [ ] Product roadmap (next 12-18 months)
- [ ] Architecture overview (scalability, reliability)
- [ ] Security posture (SOC 2, penetration testing, data handling)

### Technology
- [ ] Proprietary technology assessment
- [ ] Technical differentiation vs. open-source alternatives
- [ ] Technical debt assessment (Series B+)
- [ ] Infrastructure and cloud spend trajectory
- [ ] AI/ML model performance and data pipeline (if applicable)

### IP
- [ ] Patent portfolio (filed, granted, pending)
- [ ] Trade secrets and proprietary data assets
- [ ] Open-source dependency risk
- [ ] IP assignment confirmation (all founders/employees)

---

## 3. Financial Due Diligence

### Historical Financials
- [ ] Revenue (MRR/ARR breakdown, recognized vs. contracted)
- [ ] Gross margin and COGS breakdown
- [ ] Operating expenses by category
- [ ] Cash flow statement
- [ ] Balance sheet (cash, debt, working capital)

### Projections
- [ ] 3-5 year financial model with key assumptions
- [ ] Revenue build (bottoms-up: customers x ACV, or usage-based)
- [ ] Expense plan (hiring plan, R&D, S&M, G&A)
- [ ] Path to profitability / cash flow breakeven
- [ ] Scenario analysis (bull / base / bear)

### Unit Economics
- [ ] CAC (blended and by channel)
- [ ] LTV and LTV/CAC ratio
- [ ] Payback period
- [ ] Gross margin per customer
- [ ] Contribution margin

### Cap Table
- [ ] Current cap table (fully diluted)
- [ ] Option pool size and utilization
- [ ] Prior round terms and investor rights
- [ ] Pro forma cap table post-investment
- [ ] SAFEs/convertible notes outstanding

---

## 4. Legal Due Diligence

### Corporate
- [ ] Certificate of incorporation and amendments
- [ ] Bylaws and operating agreements
- [ ] Board minutes (last 12 months)
- [ ] Shareholder agreements and voting agreements
- [ ] Good standing certificates

### IP & Contracts
- [ ] IP assignment agreements (all employees and contractors)
- [ ] Key customer contracts (top 5-10)
- [ ] Key vendor/supplier agreements
- [ ] Partnership and licensing agreements
- [ ] Non-compete and non-solicitation agreements

### Compliance & Risk
- [ ] Pending or threatened litigation
- [ ] Regulatory compliance status
- [ ] Data privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA if applicable)
- [ ] Insurance coverage (D&O, E&O, cyber)
- [ ] Tax compliance and outstanding liabilities

---

## 5. Team Due Diligence

### Founders
- [ ] Background checks
- [ ] Reference calls (3+ per founder for Series A+)
- [ ] Founder vesting schedule and cliff status
- [ ] Full-time commitment confirmation
- [ ] Founder dynamics and role clarity

### Organization
- [ ] Org chart and key personnel
- [ ] Key person risk assessment
- [ ] Compensation benchmarking (reasonableness)
- [ ] Employee satisfaction / Glassdoor signals
- [ ] Hiring plan and ability to recruit

### Culture & Governance
- [ ] Board composition and governance practices
- [ ] Information rights and reporting cadence
- [ ] Diversity and inclusion practices
- [ ] Employee equity plan details

---

## Stage-Specific Depth Guide

| Area | Seed (1-2 wks) | Series A (2-4 wks) | Series B+ (4-8 wks) |
|------|----------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Market | Desktop research | + Expert calls (2-3) | + Commissioned research |
| Customers | Founder references | 3-5 customer calls | 5-10 calls + churn analysis |
| Technical | Product demo | + Architecture review | + Code audit / tech DD firm |
| Financial | Basic model review | Detailed model build | + Quality of earnings |
| Legal | Basic corporate | Standard legal DD | Full legal DD + IP counsel |
| Team | Founder references | 3+ references each | + Management assessment |
