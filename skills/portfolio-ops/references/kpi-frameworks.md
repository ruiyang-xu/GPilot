# KPI Frameworks by Sector and Stage

## SaaS / Software

| KPI | Definition | Seed Benchmark | Series A | Series B+ |
|-----|-----------|---------------|----------|-----------|
| ARR | Annual Recurring Revenue | >$100K | $1-5M | $5-20M+ |
| MRR Growth | Month-over-month MRR growth | >15% MoM | >10% MoM | >5% MoM |
| NDR | Net Dollar Retention | >100% | >110% | >120% |
| Gross Churn | Revenue lost from churned customers | <5% monthly | <3% monthly | <2% monthly |
| Logo Churn | % customers lost per month | <8% | <5% | <3% |
| CAC | Customer Acquisition Cost | Tracked | <12mo payback | <18mo payback |
| LTV | Lifetime Value of Customer | Estimated | >3x CAC | >5x CAC |
| LTV/CAC | Efficiency ratio | >3x | >3x | >4x |
| CAC Payback | Months to recoup CAC | <18 months | <12 months | <18 months |
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | >60% | >70% | >75% |
| Magic Number | Net new ARR / prior quarter S&M spend | >0.5 | >0.75 | >1.0 |
| Burn Multiple | Net Burn / Net New ARR | <3x | <2x | <1.5x |
| Rule of 40 | Revenue growth % + profit margin % | N/A | Trending | >40 |

## Fintech

| KPI | Definition | Early Stage | Growth |
|-----|-----------|-------------|--------|
| TPV | Total Payment Volume | Growing | >$1B/yr |
| Take Rate | Revenue / TPV | 0.5-3% | Stable or growing |
| Revenue | Net revenue after interchange | Growing >3x | Growing >50% |
| Default/Loss Rate | Credit losses / portfolio | <2% | <1.5% |
| NIM | Net Interest Margin (lending) | Positive | >3% |
| CAC Payback | | <12 months | <18 months |
| Regulatory Status | Licenses, compliance | In progress | Secured |

## Deeptech / Hardware

| KPI | Definition | Early | Growth |
|-----|-----------|-------|--------|
| R&D Milestones | Technical milestones achieved | On track | Ahead/on track |
| IP Portfolio | Patents filed/granted | Filing | >5 granted |
| Grant Funding | Non-dilutive capital | Secured | Ongoing |
| Partnerships | Strategic/industry partners | LOIs | Revenue-generating |
| Unit Cost | Cost to produce per unit | Declining | At target |
| Pilot Customers | Paid pilot deployments | First pilots | Converting to contracts |

## Universal Red Flags (Any Stage/Sector)
- Burn rate increasing faster than revenue
- Runway <6 months without fundraise plan
- Key executive departures
- Declining engagement/retention metrics
- Customer concentration >30% in single customer
- Flat or declining growth for 2+ consecutive quarters
