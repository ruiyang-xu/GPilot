# LinkedIn Formatting Guide

## Platform Characteristics
- Professional audience: VCs, founders, LPs, tech executives
- English is the primary language for LinkedIn distribution
- Mix of desktop and mobile consumption
- Algorithm favors engagement (comments > likes > shares)
- "See more" fold at ~210 characters (mobile) / ~300 characters (desktop)

## Post Formats

### 1. Text Post (for Market Notes, GP Letters)
- **Hook** (first 2 lines — before "See more" fold): Must be compelling enough to click
  - Start with a bold claim, surprising data point, or provocative question
  - NOT "I just published a new article about..."
  - Example: "Everyone is talking about AI agents. Nobody is talking about the infrastructure bottleneck that will determine who wins."
- **Body**: 800-1,300 characters (sweet spot for engagement)
- **Structure**: Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences), line breaks between each
- **Call to action**: End with a question to drive comments
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant tags at the end
  - Examples: #VentureCapital #AI #TechInvesting #StartupEcosystem #DeepTech

### 2. Document/Carousel Post (for Sector Deep Dives, Thematic Research)
- Upload the report as a PDF document (LinkedIn native carousel)
- Create a slide-deck-style PDF: key charts + insights (10-15 slides)
- Each slide: 1 key point + 1 visual, readable at mobile scale
- First slide = compelling cover with title
- Last slide = fund branding + CTA ("Follow for more research")
- Accompanying text: 3-4 line summary + the hook

### 3. Article (LinkedIn Newsletter — for Company Teardowns)
- Full article format for longer-form content
- 1,000-2,500 words (LinkedIn articles support full HTML)
- Include images inline
- Cross-link to other articles in the series
- Newsletter subscribers get email notification

## Formatting Best Practices

### Text Styling
- Use line breaks liberally (each point on its own line)
- Use → ↳ • ◆ for visual structure (no markdown support in posts)
- Bold text: not natively supported, but CAPS for emphasis (sparingly)
- Numbers at the start of lines draw attention: "3 things I learned..."

### Hooks That Work for VC Content
- **Data hook**: "The average Series A in AI just hit $18M. Here's why that number is misleading."
- **Contrarian hook**: "Unpopular opinion: Most AI startups are actually services companies."
- **Story hook**: "Last week I passed on a deal. The founder's response taught me more than the pitch."
- **Framework hook**: "I evaluate every deal through this 3-question filter:"
- **Question hook**: "What's the #1 mistake first-time VCs make in diligence?"

### Engagement Optimization
- Post between 7:00-9:00 AM or 5:00-7:00 PM (US time zones)
- Tuesday-Thursday are highest engagement days
- Reply to every comment within 2 hours (drives algorithm boost)
- Tag relevant people/companies (sparingly, only when genuinely relevant)
- Avoid external links in the main post (kills reach); put links in first comment

## Content Adaptation (EN Report → LinkedIn)

### Market Note → Text Post
- Extract the sharpest insight as the hook
- Compress analysis to 3-5 bullet points
- Add the "so what for founders/investors" angle
- Link to full report in first comment

### Company Teardown → Article or Carousel
- If <2,000 words: LinkedIn Article
- If >2,000 words: Create a 10-slide carousel PDF with key insights
- Always include the bull/bear case — drives comments

### Thematic Research → Carousel + Text Post
- Carousel: 10-12 slides covering key frameworks and data
- Text post: the contrarian thesis in 5-6 punchy lines

### Sector Deep Dive → Carousel
- This is too long for an article. Create a "Key Findings" carousel (12-15 slides)
- Each slide: 1 finding + 1 chart/data point
- Link to full report in comments

### GP Letter → Text Post
- Most natural format for LinkedIn
- the GP's personal voice translates well to the platform
- Keep it human — LinkedIn rewards authenticity

## Chinese Content on LinkedIn
- Post Chinese version as a separate post (not same day — space by 1-2 days)
- Or: English post with Chinese translation in a PDF carousel
- Chinese LinkedIn audience is smaller but highly engaged for cross-border content
- Use bilingual hashtags: #风险投资 #VentureCapital

## Profile Optimization (the GP's LinkedIn)
- Headline: "{Fund Name} | GP | Writing about tech investing"
- Featured section: Pin the 3 best research pieces
- Creator mode: Enable for newsletter access
- Consistent posting cadence: 2-3x per week minimum
