# WeChat 公众号 Formatting Guide

## Platform Constraints
- WeChat Official Account articles are mobile-first (80%+ read on phone)
- Rich text editor with limited HTML support
- Images are the primary visual medium (no embedded charts/iframes)
- Maximum article length: technically unlimited, but engagement drops after 5,000 Chinese characters
- No external links (links to non-WeChat domains show as plain text)

## Article Structure

### Cover
- Cover image: 900×500px, compelling visual
- Title: ≤22 Chinese characters (gets truncated otherwise)
- Abstract: ≤120 characters (shows in subscription preview)

### Header Template
```
{Fund Logo}
{Publication Series Name} | {Issue Number}
{Date}
```

### Body Formatting

**Headings**:
- H1: Article title only (set in WeChat editor, not in body)
- H2: Major sections — bold, larger font, with a colored left border accent
- H3: Subsections — bold, regular size

**Text**:
- Font size: 16px body text (standard for mobile readability)
- Line height: 1.75 (generous for Chinese text)
- Paragraph spacing: 15px between paragraphs
- Color: #333333 (dark gray, not pure black — easier on eyes)
- Key data/numbers: highlighted in brand color (#1A365D) or bold

**Emphasis**:
- Bold for key terms and data points
- Color highlight (brand teal #2C7A7B) for the most critical insight per section
- Block quotes (gray background) for pull quotes or key takeaways

**Images**:
- Full-width (900px) for charts and frameworks
- Center-aligned with caption below
- Caption format: "图X: {description} | 数据来源: {source}"

**Data Tables**:
- Convert to image format (WeChat's native table support is inconsistent across devices)
- Alternatively: use well-formatted text blocks with monospace alignment

### Key Insight Boxes
Use a styled blockquote for the 2-3 most important takeaways:
```
💡 核心观点
{One-sentence key insight in bold}
{2-3 sentences of supporting context}
```

### Footer Template
```
───────────
{Fund Name} Research
作者: the GP
{Brief 1-line fund description}
{QR code for following the account}

免责声明: 本文仅代表作者个人观点，不构成投资建议。
Disclaimer: Views expressed are the author's own and do not constitute investment advice.
```

## Publication Types — WeChat Optimization

### Market Note (快评) — Target: 2,000-3,000 字
- Open with the event in 1 sentence
- Core analysis in 3-5 short paragraphs
- 1-2 charts maximum
- End with clear takeaway
- Reading time: 3-5 minutes

### Company Teardown (公司拆解) — Target: 5,000-8,000 字
- Narrative opening (场景化)
- Break into clearly labeled sections
- 4-6 charts/tables
- Include a "一句话总结" (one-sentence summary) box at the top
- Reading time: 10-15 minutes

### Thematic Research (主题研究) — Target: 4,000-6,000 字
- Lead with the contrarian thesis
- Use numbered frameworks ("三个关键趋势", "五个投资主题")
- 3-5 charts
- Reading time: 8-12 minutes

### Sector Deep Dive (行业深度) — Target: 6,000-10,000 字
- Split into 2 parts if >8,000 字 (上篇/下篇)
- Heavy on charts and market maps (8-12 visuals)
- Include a summary table at the top
- Reading time: 15-25 minutes

### GP Letter (GP来信) — Target: 2,000-3,000 字
- Personal, conversational tone
- Minimal charts (0-2)
- More white space, shorter paragraphs
- Reading time: 5-8 minutes

## WeChat SEO & Distribution
- Title keywords: include the company/sector name for searchability
- First 30 characters are critical (preview text)
- Publish timing: Tuesday-Thursday, 8:00-9:00 AM or 8:00-9:00 PM (peak reading times)
- Cross-post to "看一看" (Wow/See) for viral potential
- Include 3-5 relevant tags

## Language Notes for WeChat
- Use simplified Chinese (简体中文)
- Shorter sentences than formal Chinese writing
- More conversational tone than academic papers
- Data points in Arabic numerals, not Chinese numerals: "47%" not "四十七个百分点"
- English technical terms acceptable and expected for this audience
